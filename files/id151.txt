        <h2>DESCRIPTION OF BUSINESS</h2>
        Oil and gas extraction
        
        <h2>CORPORATE HEADQUARTERS</h2>
        Stavanger, Norway
        
        <h2>NATIONALITY OF FOREIGN OFFICIALS</h2>
        Peru<br />
        
        <h2>SUMMARY OF ALLEGATIONS</h2>
        Norwegian authorities are investigating allegations that DPI paid bribes to Peruvian officials through various intermediaries, including local attorneys, in connection with the company's application for oil production licenses in the country.  <br /><br />Peruvian authorities apparently plan to charge Jostein Kjerstad, DPI's chairman of the board, in connection with the matter.<br /><br />
