        <h1>DAE-A EASTERN AIR CO.</h1>
        
        <h2>ENFORCEMENT AGENCY</h2>
        Korea: Incheon District Prosecutor's Office<br />
        
        <h2>ENTITIES/INDIVIDUALS INVOLVED</h2>
        DAE-A Eastern Air Co., a Korean logistics company<br />Su-seong Hwang, representative of the Korea branch of China Eastern Airlines<br />Dae-bong Hwang, representative director of Dae-a Eastern Air Co., Ltd.<br />Director of H Tour, a travel agency
        
        <h2>DESCRIPTION OF BUSINESS</h2>
        Logistics; travel agency
        
        <h2>CORPORATE HEADQUARTERS</h2>
        Incheon, South Korea
        
        <h2>NATIONALITY OF FOREIGN OFFICIALS</h2>
        China<br />
        
        <h2>SUMMARY OF ALLEGATIONS</h2>
        Between May 2006 and January 2011, the representative of the Korea branch of China Eastern Airlines, Su-Seong Hwang, provided a Korean logistics company, Dae-a Eastern Air Co., Ltd with transport services at a reduced fee, and accepted 5.3 billion Korean won from the representative director of Dae-a Eastern Air Co., Ltd. in return.<br />	<br />In addition, around January 2008, Hwang granted H Tour the right to sell China Eastern Airlines airline tickets in Korea and accepted 1.4 billion Korean won in return.<br /><br />Dae-bong Hwang is also charged with embezzling 37 billion Korean won in company funds under the false pretext of paying China Eastern Airlines transport fees, evading corporate taxes in the amount of 7.9 billion Korean won.  It is claimed that Hwang purchased a luxury apartment in China valued at   670 million Korean won with the embezzled funds.

        <h2>HOW CONDUCT WAS DISCOVERED</h2>
        Unspecified. 
        
        <h2>ENFORCEMENT RESULT</h2>
        On 2 May 2011, Su-seong Hwang, was indicted and detained.<br /><br />On 13 May 2011, Dae-bong Hwang and the representative director of H Tour were indicted and released. <br />
        
        <h2>KEY TAKEAWAYS</h2>
        <ul><li>This appears to be the first Korean foreign bribery case involving a Chinese government official and the first involving a foreign official in Korean jurisdiction.  </li></ul>
