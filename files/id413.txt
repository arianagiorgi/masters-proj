        <h2>SUMMARY OF ALLEGATIONS</h2>
        BG Group was one of four major oil companies involved in Karachaganak Petroleum Operating B.V. (KPO), a consortium formed for the purpose of developing the Karachaganak oil and gas condensate field in northwestern Kazakhstan, that was reported to have initiated an internal investigation after a whistleblower sent them an email in March 2012 alleging improper payments to Kazakh customs officials.  The email alleged that the members of the consortium, Chevron, ENI, Lukoil and BG Group, have been using DHL to make improper payments to Kazakh customs authorities since 2004.  The purpose of the payments is to allow shipments to pass through customs in spite of small paperwork errors; without the payments, the errors could have caused significant delays in the importation of necessary supplies by these companies.
