        <h2>SUMMARY OF ALLEGATIONS</h2>
        According to press reports on October 30, 2011, ThyssenKrupp is conducting an internal investigation into bribery allegations involving its submarine building subsidiary, ThyssenKrupp HDW, in South Korea.   The subsidiary may have made improper payments through South Korean businessman Chung Eui Sung's company, UBMTECH Ltd., in order to secure two submarine contracts valued at EUR 2.5 million.  UBMTECH reportedly serves at the local agent for HDW's joint venture in South Korea, Marine Force International (MFI) (the other JV partner is Ferrostaal AG).  HDW allegedly made over EUR 90 million in provisions to UBMTECH since 2000.

        This investigation reportedly stems from German prosecutors' bribery investigation into Tognum AG's activities in South Korea. The prosecutors' probe has reportedly widened to include ThyssenKrupp's activities in the country.
