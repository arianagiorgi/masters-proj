        <h2>SUMMARY OF ALLEGATIONS</h2>
        On 10 January 2013, farm equipment manufacturer Deere & Co. said the Securities and Exchange Commission ended a bribery investigation of the company without taking action.<br /><br />The Wall Street Journal reported in August 2011 that regulators were investigating whether the company made payments in Russia and surrounding countries that violated the FCPA.<br /><br />“Deere & Company has been notified in a letter from the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission Enforcement Division staff that it has completed an investigation of Deere and does not plan to pursue any enforcement action,” the company said in a news release.
