        Belgian authorities are reportedly investigating Belgian utility firm Tractebel Engineering SA (a subsidiary of France's GDF Suez) in connection with bribery allegations involving pipeline projects in Kazakhstan.  Tractebel allegedly paid GBP 3.5 million in bribes to Kazakh officials through three middlemen to secure a GBP 39 million pipeline deal in 2002.
