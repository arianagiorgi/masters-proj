        <h2>SUMMARY OF ALLEGATIONS</h2>
        On 12 November 2012, managers of Farmal were arrested in Croatia on suspicion of corruption.  The Croatian Office for Suppression of Corruption and Organized Crime ("USKOK") told reporters that police were investigating 26 employees of a pharmaceutical company, 49 doctors and one pharmacist suspected of bribery, and that 350 physicians in total may be under investigation.  Although USKOK did not name the company targeted by the investigation, media sources named Farmal, saying that employees of Farmal are being invesigated for giving bribes to doctors in exchange for prescribing Farmal products.
