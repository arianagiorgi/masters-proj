        <h2>SUMMARY OF ALLEGATIONS</h2>
        According to press reports, in 2001 Italian prosecutors brought charges against the manager of Vivendi Acqua, Alain Maetz, and the former Milan city council president, Massimo De Carolis, for bribery in 1998 in connection with a USD 100 million contract secured by Vivendi Acqua to construct a water treatment plant in Milan.  Vivendi Acqua allegedly paid a USD 2 million bribe to secure the deal.
