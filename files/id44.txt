        <h1>NISHIMATSU CONSTRUCTION</h1>
        
        <h2>ENFORCEMENT AGENCY</h2>
        Japan: Tokyo District Public Prosecutors Office <br />
        
        <h2>ENTITIES/INDIVIDUALS INVOLVED</h2>
        Nishimatsu Construction Co., Ltd. ("Nishimatsu")<br />Italian-Thai Development Public Co. ("ITDP")
        
        <h2>DESCRIPTION OF BUSINESS</h2>
        Infrastructure and construction
        
        <h2>CORPORATE HEADQUARTERS</h2>
        Tokyo, Japan
        
        <h2>NATIONALITY OF FOREIGN OFFICIALS</h2>
        Thailand<br />
        
        <h2>SUMMARY OF ALLEGATIONS</h2>
        According to news agency reports, in 2003 Nishimatsu and ITDP, Nishimatsu's local partner in Thailand, gave bribes of more than JPY 400,000,000 (USD 3,600,000) to foreign officials of the Bangkok Metropolitan Administration in return for favors to secure a USD 77,500,000 drainage tunnel construction project in Thailand.  The TDPPO is continuously investigating this Nishimatsu case.
