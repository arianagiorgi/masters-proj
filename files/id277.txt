        <h2>SUMMARY OF ALLEGATIONS</h2>
        According to press reports, in early 2009 the UK Serious Fraud Office began investigating Freeport plc for possible bribery of Portuguese officials in connection with the building of a large shopping complex in Portugal.<br /><br />On 13 November 2009, the SFO released a press release that the investigation had been closed.
