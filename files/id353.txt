        <h2>SUMMARY OF ALLEGATIONS</h2>
        Israeli authorities are investigating whether Omri and Gilad Sharon, the sons of former prime minister Ariel Sharon, served as conduits for the passage of bribe monies to Ariel Sharon from the notorious ex-Stasi Austrian businessman, Martin Schlaff.  Schlaff allegedly transferred funds to Ariel Sharon so that the former prime minister could pay off debts he had amounted during his 2001 election campaign.  Sharon was reportedly to promote Schlaff's business interests in Israel in return.  <br /><br />Schlaff himself has avoided visiting Israel during the course of the decade-long investigation due to a fear of being arrested by Israeli police.
