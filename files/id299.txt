        <h2>SUMMARY OF ALLEGATIONS</h2>
        The Hanover Public Prosecutor's Office is investigating Vietz GmbH and its owner and managing director, Eginhard Vietz, for potential bribery of foreign government officials in various countries in order to secure business.  On August 20, 2010, German police reportedly raided Vietz's head office in Hanover, as well as its plants in the cities of Leipzig and Essen, removing computers and paper files.

        <h2>HOW CONDUCT WAS DISCOVERED</h2>
        In an interview with German business newspaper Handelsblatt, published on August 10, 2010, Eginhard Vietz stated that his company paid bribes in various countries.  The interview prompted the police raid and initiation of an investigation by the Hanover Public Prosecutor's Office.
