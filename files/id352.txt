        <h2>SUMMARY OF ALLEGATIONS</h2>
        OBB Holding AG, wholly-owned by the Republic of Austria, manages the railway and transport subsidiary companies comprising the "OBB Group", including Austrian Federal Railways ("OBB") and transport service provider, Rail Cargo Austria.<br /><br />The Vienna Public Prosecutor's Office raided OBB Group's offices in October 2010 in connection with a bribery investigation related to the privatization in 2008 of MAV Cargo, the freight arm of Hungary's state-owned railway.  OBB Group and Gysev - an Austrian/Hungarian-owned regional rail company - acquired MAV Cargo for EUR 400 million while paying a questionable EUR 7 million fee to a Budapest consultancy. <br /><br />The Hungarian authorities are reportedly investigating in coordination with the Austrians.
