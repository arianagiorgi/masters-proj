        <h2>SUMMARY OF ALLEGATIONS</h2>
        According to press reports on 5 September 2011, US regulators are investigating Motorola for possible bribery violations in connection with the company's use of an Austrian lobbyist, Alfons Mensdorff-Pouilly.  Motorola reportedly hired Pouilly in 2004 and transferred up to EUR 2.2 million to three firms controlled by him in the subsequent years, the funds used to make improper payments to key political figures in Europe and the Middle East.
