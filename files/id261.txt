        <h2>SUMMARY OF ALLEGATIONS</h2>
        ARGENTINA<br /><br />According to press reports, in 2007 Argentine authorities began investigating possible wrongdoing in connection with Skanska's win in 2005 of a contract award for a national gas distribution network project.  Skanska employees allegedly paid USD 5.4 million in bribes to government officials to secure the deal.  The Federal Criminal Court in Buenos Aires is investigating the company for possible bribery.  <br /><br />In May 2007, Argentine police arrested five Skanska managers in connection with the investigation.<br /><br /><br />BRAZIL<br /><br />According to a press release issued by Skanska, Brazil's Comptroller General (CGU) and Administrative Council of Economic Defense (CADE) announced that they would begin investigating Skanska's Brazil unit Skanska do Brasil as part of their Petrobras investigation.
