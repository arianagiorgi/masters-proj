        <h2>ENTITIES/INDIVIDUALS INVOLVED</h2>
        Patrick Orr<br />Guido Bakker<br />Arthur Scheffer<br />
        
        <h2>DESCRIPTION OF BUSINESS</h2>
        Supply of pharmaceutical products to relief agencies
        
        <h2>CORPORATE HEADQUARTERS</h2>
        United Kingdom
        
        <h2>NATIONALITY OF FOREIGN OFFICIALS</h2>
        Democratic Republic of the Congo<br />
        
        <h2>SUMMARY OF ALLEGATIONS</h2>
        Patrick Orr, a solicitor, Guido Bakker and Arthur Scheffer (two Dutch consultants) were charged in June 2009 with conspiracy to corrupt, conspiring to money launder and fraudulent trading in relation to alleged contract manipulation concerning a UN project to supply HIV and malaria drugs in the Democratic Republic of Congo.  The City of London Police have stated that the value of the contract was USD 34 million.
